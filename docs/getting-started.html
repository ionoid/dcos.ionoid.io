# Getting Started

Start your IoT development journey by first creating your Ionoid.io account, please follow the instructions below.

# Create Your Account

  • Visit the Ionoid.io dashboard
  • Click on Register now
  • Fill in the required informations
  • Accept our Terms of Service
  • Click on the Create account button

# Verify Email Address

  • Check your inbox for an email from support@ionoid.io to verify your email address

  • Click on the Verify Email link to activate your account
  • You will be redirected to the Ionoid.io dashboard dashboard to automatically activate your account

# Sign In

  • Once activated, you will be redirected to the login page, enter your credentials then click on Login

Have Questions?

We're always happy to help with IoT projects or other questions you might have! Check our documentation, contact our support team at support@ionoid.io, or our sales team sales@opendevices.io. You can also ask questions on our forums or open an issue on Github.
